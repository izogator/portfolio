04.03.01 - PROJECT POOL 1

This was another group project. We had to choose a task form a pool of different examples on different levels. Together with Lana, we chose to make "Smack The Fly" game. Our fly is making a sound while flying and there is a smack sound after hitting it. The image of the fly is changing into a dead fly after hitting it. We also put a pop-up window with an introduction text which disappears after clicking on the fly the first time and a user can pause the game by pressing the space bar. After every second succeed hit the speed of the fly rise 25%. I was responsible for all the visual parts and Lana did the coding.
