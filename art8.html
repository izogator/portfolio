04.04.01 - ONBOARDING

While moving to Denmark students are always struggling with registration and documentation work. We focused on making a solution which will make this process easier. Firstly, when settling down in Denmark students have to obtain a Residence Permit and CPR number and to accomplish that, people have to go through several steps. Our solution was a “tutorial” game where new students get knowledge about required documents while getting rewards for accomplished tasks.

We started with making a kanban chart where we could see what is there to do and what work is done. That made the process much easier. For the project, we did moodboard, style tile, persona collage and a storyboard.

We worked in 4 people team, so we divided the work. Together with Ania, we did all the visual parts - illustrations, buttons and sprites.

We got to use a lot of skills that we learned in the previous projects such as coding in HTML, CSS and JavaScript or making and editing images in Photoshop and Illustrator. I found it really educative to learn how to combine all of those skills.
